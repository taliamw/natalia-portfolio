AgriMarket

Project Overview

AgriMarket is a mobile platform connecting small-scale rural farmers directly with urban customers. The goal is to eliminate middlemen, increase farmersâ€™ earnings, and make fresh produce more accessible.

Problem

Rural farmers often sell through brokers, reducing profits and limiting control over their products. Existing e-commerce solutions are rarely optimized for rural users or local languages.

Research and Insights

Interviews with farmers revealed challenges including digital literacy, trust, and market access. Urban customers indicated interest in direct, transparent purchases and reliable delivery. This informed the design of intuitive flows, simple onboarding, and localized content.

Solution and Design Approach

AgriMarket was designed for accessibility and usability. The mobile UI enables listing, browsing, and ordering produce in a few steps. Features include Swahili language support, secure payment flows, and clear status tracking. Prototypes were iteratively tested with farmers and buyers to refine interactions.

Reflection and Next Steps

The project highlighted the importance of designing for low-tech users and building trust into digital platforms. Future work includes integrating real-time inventory, delivery logistics, and expanding outreach to more rural communities.

Outcome and Impact

AgriMarket creates a direct, transparent marketplace, improving incomes for farmers while giving consumers easier access to fresh produce. The interactive UI prototype demonstrates the feasibility and usability of the solution.
